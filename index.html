FAQ

What kind of game is Sakura?

It’s a high paced decision making game. If you make the right decisions, you continue forward. If you fail, you die.

You can die?

Yes, you can die. Once you die you are revoked access to the game and your wallet gets disconnected. The only way to play again is with a new wallet and new pass.

How does the NFTs work?

There will be 2 smart contracts deployed. 1 for Shinobi and 1 for Samurai. The Shinobi player pass only unlocks the Shinobi path and Samurai only unlocks the Samurai path.

How will you know who got the prizes first?

We will be using a couple of different features to create a breadcrumb trail during the game play itself.
