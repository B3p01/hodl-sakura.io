FAQ

What is Sakura?

It is a high paced decision making game. If you make the right decisions, you continue forward. If you fail, you die.

You can die?

Yes, you can die. Once you die, your NFT pass is revoked access to the game and your wallet will be disconnected. The only way to play again is with a new wallet and a new pass.

How do the NFTs work?

There will be two smart contracts deployed. One for Shinobi and one for Samurai. Each NFT will act as a key, unlocking either pathway!

How will you know who got the prizes first?

We will use breadcrumb trail technology to track movements and verify the first player to reach each prize.
